ABOUT US

This Web Application is developed as a part of the IIT Madras BS-Degree Project Component of the "Modern Application Development-1" course. Built with Python, Flask, SQLite3, HTML/CSS, and Bootstrap, this platform is tailored for 4-wheeler parking management.

The application has two types of users: Admin and Users. Admins can manage parking lots, view summaries, and monitor registered users. Users can seamlessly book parking spots and track their active bookings from the "Your Booking" page.
